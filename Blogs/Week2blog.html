Reflection On Internet History

The history of the internet is fascinating and complex, there are a series of milestones and developments that have transformed the way we live, work, and communicate. The internet was first developed in the 1960s as a means of connecting computers and sharing information between nodes, for only certain industries but change it develop to allow use by other industries. Then in the early 1990s, the world wide web was developed allowing for the creation of easily accessible web pages and opening the Internet to a wider audience. Just understanding and viewing the history of the internet shows how it has had a profound impact on our lives we communicate and connect with others to work and access all kinds of information. I find the history of the internet interesting to see how the internet has changed over the years and how it continues to shape our world, I look forward to how it will further evolve and impact my life and others.
