Reading: Hassenzahl & Tractinsky

In their article, Hassenzahl and Tractinsky provide an overview of the concept of user experience and propose a way to advance it. They argue that the user experience is multidimensional. This indicates that each aspect such as emotions, controls, behaviour, and context plays a role in how the user interacts with the website. They also emphasize the importance of UX, to rather show it as interactive products, services, and environments for the user. One key point that stood out to me is the subjectivity and dynamic nature of UX. They point out that UX is not just a matter of usability or functionality.
